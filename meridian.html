Body copy
Meridian is a contemporary geometric sans-serif typeface derived from the structural skeleton of Inter and recast with tighter proportions, a raised x-height, and more open apertures on key letters. The result is a typeface suited to editorial use, interfaces, and display settings where a clean but characterful voice is required.
